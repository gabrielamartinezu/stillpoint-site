Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Stillpoint is designed with your privacy as a priority. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

Data Collected

All data created within Stillpoint — including your app selections, session history, journal reflections, and usage statistics — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing leaves your device.

Screen Time & Family Controls

Stillpoint uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls framework) solely to monitor and shield apps you select. This data is managed entirely by iOS and is never accessible to us. We do not see, store, or transmit which apps you monitor.

No Analytics or Tracking

Stillpoint contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, advertising networks, or third-party trackers of any kind.

No Account Required

Stillpoint requires no account, login, or email address. There is no server-side component to this app.

Children's Privacy

Stillpoint does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected from any user, no special handling is required.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
